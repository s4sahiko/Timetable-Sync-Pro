About Sahiko.Org

Smart schedule syncing powered by Sahiko.org.

Our Mission

At Sahiko, our mission is to empower students and professionals by eliminating the logistical burden of academic scheduling. We leverage sophisticated AI-driven recognition and advanced data structuring to instantaneously convert any static timetable into a flawless, universally compatible iCalendar (.ics) format. We deliver effortless synchronization, unparalleled accuracy, and peace of mind, ensuring our users can prioritize their goals and maximize their productivity, free from the friction of manual data management.

The Technology Behind Timetable Sync Pro

Advanced OCR:
We use highly precise Optical Character Recognition specialized for complex timetable layouts to accurately extract text including days, times, subjects, and locations from your uploaded image.
AI Data Structuring:
Our proprietary AI model then cleans and maps this raw text. It intelligently validates entries, handles ambiguities, and normalizes all time and day notations into a standard digital format.
iCalendar Generation:
Finally, we compile the structured data into a fully compliant iCalendar (.ics) file, ensuring seamless integration with all major calendar applications.
